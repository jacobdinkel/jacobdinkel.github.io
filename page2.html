My Favorite Hobby

My favorite hobby is fishing. I love fishing because it is very peaceful and it takes skill and knowledge. You have to know where to position the boat and where the perfect spot to cast is. You have to make sure that your line doesn't get tangled and also know what bait to use. You have to know how far out to curve your hook and what bait to use. All of this knowledge adds up and adds to the excitement of fishing. Fishing is an addiction, it's that urge of knowing what is on the other side of your line that keeps you casting. Fishing is also very rewarding and that is why I love it.
